Mariyam Varreed

Age 87 22/12/1991

Mariyam Vareed (born 1904, Mambra) was married to Vareed. She was a devoted homemaker known for simplicity, dedication, and strong family values. She had two daughters, Mariyamma and Sosamma, whom she raised with care and discipline. Mariyamma later married Ittoop of Pynadath family, Nayathode, and Sosamma married Iteerah of the Telappilli family, Peechanikadu.

Mariyamma Ittoop

Age 60 10/02/1994

Mariyamma Ittoop (born 1934, Maickavu) was the daughter of Vareed and Mariyam Vareed. She was married to Ittoop P. M. of the Pynadath (Iriyattu) family, Nayathode. She devoted herself to her family through her care, household responsibilities, and unwavering support. They were blessed with five children: Mathew, Varghese, Joshy, Molly, and Saji. Her life reflected dedication, humility, faith, and a strong commitment to traditional family values.

Ittoop P.M.

Age 79 06/04/2009

Ittoop P. M. (born 1930, Nayathode) belonged to the Pynadath (Iriyattu) family. He was married to Mariyamma, and together they built a strong and loving family based on care, unity, and responsibility. They had five children: Mathew, Varghese, Joshy, Molly, and Saji, whom they raised with affection and moral guidance. He was engaged in agriculture and valued education, discipline, respect, and strong family relationships throughout his life. He is fondly remembered for his dedication to his family, his simple way of life, and the wisdom and guidance he shared with those around him.

Mathew P.I.

Age 54 16/06/2006

Mathew P. I., the eldest son of Ittoop P. M. and Mariyamma Ittoop, was born on October 22, 1959. He completed his schooling at MAHSS School, Nedumbassery, followed by Pre-Degree at Sree Sankara College and a Polytechnic Diploma from Govt. Polytechnic, Kalamassery. He worked as an Engineer at Travancore Chemical Manufacturing Company Ltd. He married Mary of Valiyaputhenpurayil, Niranam, on May 14, 1979, and they were blessed with a daughter, Neethu. He is remembered for his gentle personality and warm, ever-smiling presence, leaving behind lasting memories in the hearts of his loved ones.

Joshy P.I.

Age 65 10/09/2023

Joshy P. I. (born 1958) was the son of Ittoop P. M. and Mariyamma Ittoop. He studied at MAHSS School, Nedumbassery, and later pursued his higher education at Sree Sankara College,kalady.He worked at Kancor Ingredients Limited,Angamaly. He married Elizabeth and they were blessed with two sons, Sam Joshy and Mathew Joshy.He will be fondly remembered for his loving personality and the warmth he shared with everyone around him.

Varghese P.I.

Age 74 12/04/2026

Varghese P. I. was born on 10/11/1952 as the second son of Ittoop P. M. and Mariyamma Ittoop. He completed his schooling at MAHSS School, Nedumbassery, and later worked in the Maintenance Department at Cochin Shipyard Limited. He married Lissy of the Marangattu family, Vengoor, on 08/06/1981, and they were blessed with two sons, Basil P. Varghese and Jose P. Varghese.His cheerful presence and caring attitude left a lasting impression on everyone who knew him.
